Business Model

We want to build a one-stop shop for everyone involved; buyers, sellers, renters and agents. Consumers and the agents use many sites and sources to close a deal, we want to bring all of that into yurtah community.
Subscription as a Service
There are 2 million active realtors. Since consumers don't buy frequently, we want to empower agents who are responsible for 98% of deals today.
Video Production
Video use for real estate is just beginning. We provide fast and affordable video for property and personal brand.
Marketing Tools
Agents are great at relationships, soft skills, sales, but lack the marketing skills and tech. We want to automate marketing tools and analytics for agents.
Web Design
Every realtor is a small business, and their business is built on referrals and honesty. Realtors want to have a nice and accessible personal site.
Sales Commission
Once automated, we are able to represent both sides of the deal and save money. Currently, each side of the deal gets 3% each, totaling 6% of price.
DATA
Real Estate marketplace is full of DATA that can be monetized.
