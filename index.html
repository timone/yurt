Next-generation real estate marketplace

Modern platform for buying, selling and renting your home today.

Problem: Home is often the biggest financial and emotional investment for people, yet finding or selling your home today is a hassle. It is expensive and time consuming.

Problems

1
Hard to buy,sell or rent a home.
The buying or selling is low frequency (happens rarely) and high value (costs a lot), where a mistake can be very costly.
It is not only financially the largest decision for most people, but also emotionally as it involves family, career, or relocation.
You may also have to deal with 10 professionals along the way. This means it can cost anywhere from 2-10% of cost.
2
Dishonesty and Untrustworthiness
People seek honesty and trustworthiness as #1 trait in realtors. However, realtors are ranked low as trustworthy professionals, barely at 11%.
Even though there are some great agents, the industry has a bad reputation.
Worse, when 98% of people search online, they end up on a website which usually has fake listings, fake photos, and sell your phone number and email to random agents for profit.
3
Technology does not empower people
The technology has disrupted many industries, but real estate has been barely improved. The internet made it possible to have a search aggregate but, it has not significantly improved the experience. It has done the opposite, it hurts the experience by misleading consumers and hurting the realtors.
The current way of finding home sucks. Watch for more.
