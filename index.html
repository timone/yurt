Yurtah [yoor-tah] video-based real estate marketplace

Problem:
The current system is outdated, where inconsistent technology adaption creates inefficiency. The market is full of unharnessed real estate data opportunities.

  • Photoshop photos create mistrust and frustration
  • High fee structures (months rent or 6-10% of sales)
  • The slow complex system creates problems for consumers and agents
  • Zillow/Streeteasy abuses market monopoly with fees and ‘premier agent’ angering realtors
  • Slow communication between all parties involved
  • Inconsistent industry adaption of technology: mobile apps, video, chat, social media and calendar features
  • Unharnessed Data

Solution: Millennial-friendly platform with verified videos that creates a single source for the entire real estate ecosystem.

Competition

The competition is Zillow and Redfin.
Zillow/Streeteasy
Agents are unhappy with Zillow's Premier Agent program. The quality of leads are low, the ROI is unjustified because of the minimum monthly subscription of $1,500/month. We are better because we are agent friendly, from giving the agents Personal Profile, to allowing buyers directly connect to them via chat! We are transparent and honor peoples largest purchase. We also don't charge for listings and work with REBNY. We also take direct listing feed from RLS.
Redfin
Redfin is a mixture of tech + brokerages. We are better because there entire industry of real estate needs a marketplace. Having a central platform will increase the cohesion and efficiency in transactions by keeping everything in one place. Since we empower agents and provide personal profiles, video editing and calendar booking features, any agents using our platform can focus on giving the best customer service, while allowing us to do the boring admin and marketing stuff.

Growth Strategy

Yurtah is the first modern video-based real estate platform. Consumers attract Agents and Agents bring in Consumers. By addressing the requests of both parties we can create a customer base that feeds itself. We are starting with the New York market as it's the one we know best but the entire site has been built to scale, from multiple markets to different languages. Our competitors are slow and outdated while our product competitiveness is adaptive through a far cheaper lean programming team designed to turn customer feedback into viable changes. A younger home-buying generation needs a modern real estate marketplace.
