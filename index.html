Next-generation real estate marketplace for buying, selling, and renting a home

Problem:
Home is often the biggest financial and emotional investment for people, but finding or selling your home today is an expensive, time consuming, and just plain frustrating.

Problems

1
Industry Low Consumer Satisfaction and High Fee Structure
  • Websites such as Zillow and StreetEasy undercut agents by selling their leads for a price
  • Daily listing fees
  • Prohibitive fees in excess of 3% for both the buying and selling agents on home sales
  • Industry wide low consumer satisfaction
  • Fake and stale listings that waste time for users or change expectations
2
Technology does not empower Agents or Consumers
  • Industry slow to adapt to modern disruptors: mobile apps, video, chat, and calendar
  • 90% of sales are closed with the help of agents/brokers, but the current technology available to them is lagging
  • Fake and stale listings that waste time for users or change expectations

Market Validation

One in every 5 Americans move every year. 97% of first time home buyers start their search online, and most end up on Zillow, which sells your private info and ruins your home search.
Over 90 of these transactions happen with the help of a realtor, and these realtors are also getting treated terribly by Zillow by undercutting their business and selling back them their own leads.
Agents

Competition

The competition is Zillow and Redfin.
Zillow/Streeteasy
Agents are unhappy with Zillow's Premier Agent program. The quality of leads are low, the ROI is unjustified because of the minimum monthly subscription of $1,500/month. We are better because we are agent friendly, from giving the agents Personal Profile, to allowing buyers directly connect to them via chat! We are transparent and honor peoples largest purchase. We also don't charge for listings and work with REBNY. We also take direct listing feed from RLS.
Redfin
Redfin is a mixture of tech + brokerages. We are better because there entire industry of real estate needs a marketplace. Having a central platform will increase the cohesion and efficiency in transactions by keeping everything in one place. Since we empower agents and provide personal profiles, video editin and calendar booking features, any agents using our platform can focus on giving the best customer service, while allowing us to do the boring admin and marketing stuff.
